About

I love to watch car racing as Car Racing is not just about 20+ car's running round the track several times, it's the Andrenaline Rush that all of us feel watching a race getting unfold, The mechanical marvel that we have achieved, The limits that we have crossed (several times) It's not easy for a piece of metal to reach 300kmph/190mph it's us who made it possible.

Car Racing is just not all Cars and no Human, It's also about the Ability to Pilot that car way above limits of ourselfs & that cars' which is made possible by our own.

Driving a car is not just about understanding the mechanical aspects of driving a car, it's about controlling something on your own in various critical conditions, it's also about enjoying yourself handle that piece of metal unfolding it's marvel which indeed what it was made for.

Reaching 250kmph is really a thrilling experience & some people do it to support there livelyhood.
It's really mind blowing. How we, as a human are able to reach such milestones.
